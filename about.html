Who We Are

ABOUT

True to our name—Rivka, meaning “to tie” or “to moderate”—our studio serves as a vital connective tissue.

We don’t just discover talent; we cross-pollinate it. By pairing emerging writers, directors, and visionaries from both sides of the Atlantic, we break down geographic barriers to produce bold, high-concept, culturally resonant films.

Founded in 2026, Rivka Studios operates at the intersection of two worlds—London and New York—where different perspectives collide and something entirely new is born.

Our vision is guided by a single word: timshel. Thou mayest. The power of choice lives at the heart of every story we choose to tell.
