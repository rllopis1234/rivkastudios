Who We Are

ABOUT

True to our name—Rivka, meaning “to tie” or “to moderate”—our studio serves as a vital connective tissue.

We don’t just discover talent; we cross-pollinate it. By pairing emerging writers, directors, and visionaries from both sides of the Atlantic, we break down geographic barriers to produce bold, high-concept, culturally resonant films.

Founded in 2026, Rivka Studios operates at the intersection of two worlds—London and New York—where different perspectives collide and something entirely new is born.

Our vision is guided by a single word: timshel. Thou mayest. The power of choice lives at the heart of every story we choose to tell.

01

Connective Tissue

We exist to bind talent across geographies, cultures, and disciplines. The seams between worlds are where the most interesting stories live.

02

Cross-Atlantic Vision

With roots in London and New York, we pair emerging writers, directors, and visionaries from both sides of the Atlantic to break down geographic barriers.

03

High Concept, Human Core

Bold ideas anchored in genuine human truth. We don’t chase trends—we back filmmakers who have something irreplaceable to say.

Timshel

“Thou mayest.” Every creative decision at Rivka is an act of freedom. We give filmmakers the choice to make the work only they can make.
