How We Work

FLOW

Our process is built around a single conviction: the best films emerge when the right people find each other at the right moment. Flow is how we make that happen—deliberately, transparently, and with deep respect for the creative process.

01

Discovery

We actively seek out emerging writers, directors, and producers on both sides of the Atlantic. Through festivals, submissions, and direct relationships, we identify voices that have something irreplaceable to say. Our open submissions are always active—we read everything.

02

Development

Once we connect with a project, we enter a collaborative development phase. We work alongside writers and directors to sharpen the vision—never to dilute it. This stage is unhurried. We believe in giving stories the time they need to become fully themselves.

03

Cross-Pollination

Our defining move: pairing talent from different worlds. A London-based cinematographer with a New York writer. A European director with a Caribbean producer. These collisions aren’t accidental—they’re engineered to create something neither party could have made alone.

04

Production

We produce with full creative autonomy for our filmmakers. Our role is infrastructure, relationships, and resources—not creative control. On set and off, we stay close but light-handed. The film belongs to its makers.

05

Distribution & Release

We approach each release as a cultural event, not a transaction. From festival strategy to theatrical positioning to streaming partnerships, we build release campaigns that match the ambition of the work itself.

06

Ongoing Relationship

Our partnerships don’t end at release. Rivka Studios is built for long-term creative relationships. We grow with our filmmakers across multiple projects, multiple territories, multiple chapters of their careers.
